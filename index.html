Research Scientist, Meta

Email CV Scholar GitHub Twitter LinkedIn

I am a Research Scientist at Meta, passionate about making digital humans a reality.

Previously, I completed my Masters (2017-2019) and Ph.D. (2019-2023) in Robotics from Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Kris Kitani. Before that, I received my Bachelors (2013-2017) in Computer Science from IIT Bombay. My Ph.D. was supported by the Amazon Fellowship Program.

My research interests are in large-scale self-supervised learning, 3D modeling, and controllable interactive video diffusion. If you are interested in interning with me, feel free to reach out via email with your resume and research interests.
